A system that "manages" a garage. The garage handles five types of vehicles: regular motorcycle, electric motorcycle, standard car, electric car and truck.

The app purpose is to help car work shops to manage the garage.
The vehicle types app supports: electric/fuel Car, electric/fuel Motorcycle, Trunk.
The app supports two login types: locally login or login with mongoDB database.
The app includes several actions you can take:
Add new vehicle to the system.
Show all vehicles in the garage.
Change vehicle state ( states are: in repair, repaired, paid).
Wheels air blowing.
Refule vehicle.
Charge vehicle (for electric vheicles).
Customer card details.
